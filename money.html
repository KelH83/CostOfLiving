Did you know you can make money whilst spending money?

With cashback sites you could make hundreds of pounds just by shopping. Sign up to cashback sites and everytime you are shopping online check and see if you could get cashback.

For example It might not seem like much but getting 40p back on your weekly shop would amount to £20.80 over the course of a year multiply that by every purchase you make and the cash soon builds up.

There are lots of other ways to make extra money such as taking part in research, many researchers offer cash or vouchers as a reward. For example, the autism research centre often rewards research participants, they always need new participants, so if you are autistic or the parent of an autistic child then sign up.

You could make extra money by selling your unwanted items on second hand sites such as ebay and vinted.

You could also become a secret shopper, someone who is paid to shop and give feedback to the company on their experience

Or you could do survey's to earn money.
