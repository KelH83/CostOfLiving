Activities with kids don't need to cost a fortune and keeping them entertained during school breaks can be difficult. Here are some tips and links to help you find free or low cost things to do with your children

Parks are a great way to entertain children. Let them burn off their energy kicking a ball about or playing tennis. Bring a picnic along and make a day of it. For more ideas on low cost activities visit the make time to play site.

If your family is a fan of the great outdoors then consider a national trust membership. There is a yearly cost but in return you get alot of great benefits.

Check out your local facebook pages as there are often free or low cost activities available via places like churches, local youth groups, sports groups, garden centres and schools.

If you shop with tescos and collect clubcard points, check out their rewards. You could book some great days out in exchange for your clubcard vouchers.

If you can afford it there are lots of local days out and activities available. Take a look on the days out site for more info.
